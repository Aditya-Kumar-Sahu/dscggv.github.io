About Us

GirlScript Bilaspur is a chapter of Girlscript India foundation, which is a section-8 non-profit company registered under government of India on the 2nd of June,2017. It is a city organization that is helping technology-challenged people learn and understand tech and its benefits. Some might find that technology is not their cup of tea, but here at Girlscript Bilaspur we believe that technology is by everybody, for everybody.
